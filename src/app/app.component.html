Highly customizable skeleton loader component
My first npm published library

I made this Angular skeleton loader component library with customization and adaptability in mind. Don't be afraid to make your loader unique!

You can customize everything from colors of the background and the moving highlight, to the horizontal alignment of the loader relative to its container. You can change even the border-radius or the line-height size in order to replicate as much as possible the final loaded interface of your page. I hope this library will add to your app that small detail that makes the difference for your users.
